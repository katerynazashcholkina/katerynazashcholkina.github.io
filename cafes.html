The Brown Bean Café — Work-Friendly Spot in Haarlem

The Brown Bean

A minimalist café in gray and white tones, designed for freelancers and students. Calm and focused atmosphere.

Opening hours: Mon–Fri: 07:30–18:00, Sat–Sun: 09:00–17:00

Wi-Fi quality: Very fast, ideal for video calls, 5/5

Noise level: Generally quiet, soft background music

Power outlets availability: Plenty of outlets along the walls

Address: Kruisweg 102, Haarlem

Brew & Work Café — Work-Friendly Spot in Haarlem

Brew & Work

A warm café with wooden interiors and brown-green accents. Popular with locals but still comfortable for working.

Opening hours: Mon–Sat: 08:00–22:00, Sun: Closed

Wi-Fi quality: Reliable, 4/5

Noise level: Moderate, especially in the afternoon

Power outlets availability: Limited, mostly near the back tables

Address: Kleine Houtstraat 23, Haarlem

Cafe Green Haven — Work-Friendly Spot in Haarlem

Cafe Green Haven

A modern café filled with plants, cozy seating, and large windows. Perfect for quiet laptop work.

Opening hours: Mon–Sun: 08:00–20:00

Wi-Fi quality: Fast and stable, 4/5

Noise level: Quiet in the morning and afternoon, slightly busier in the evening

Power outlets availability: Available near most tables

Address: Gedempte Oude Gracht 45, Haarlem
