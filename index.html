Working from Cafés in Haarlem

Is it okay to work from a café for several hours if I only order one drink?

Etiquette and tips for long café work sessions

Most cafés are fine if you order something and don’t take up too much space. If you stay long, consider getting a snack or a second drink to support the place.

Optimal Times to Work in Haarlem Spaces

What’s the best time of day to find a free seat in cafés or libraries?

When cafés and libraries are least crowded

Mornings (before 10:30) and early afternoons (around 13:00–15:00) are usually the quietest. Evenings can get busy in cafés.
