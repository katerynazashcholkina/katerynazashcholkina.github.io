Kleine Hout Library

A cozy mid-sized library with a mix of traditional and modern furniture. Suitable for reading or casual laptop work.

Opening hours: Mon–Fri: 09:00–19:00, Sat: 10:00–16:00, Sun: Closed

Wi-Fi quality: Good speed, stable, 4/5

Noise level: Quiet, with occasional chatter

Power outlets availability: Available at some tables

Address: Kleine Houtstraat 78, Haarlem

Haarlem Central Library

A spacious, high-tech library with modern furniture, quiet study zones, and digital resources for remote work.

Opening hours: Mon–Fri: 08:00–20:00, Sat–Sun: 10:00–18:00

Wi-Fi quality: High-speed, very stable, 5/5

Noise level: Very quiet, ideal for focused work

Power outlets availability: Plentiful at desks and study rooms

Address: Stationsplein 4, Haarlem

Old Town Library

A charming old library with wooden shelves, warm brown tones, and a classic atmosphere. Limited high-tech facilities but still a peaceful workspace.

Opening hours: Mon–Sat: 08:00–18:00, Sun: Closed

Wi-Fi quality: Basic internet, may be slower, 3/5

Noise level: Very quiet

Power outlets availability: Limited, mostly near main desks

Address: Grote Markt 12, Haarlem
